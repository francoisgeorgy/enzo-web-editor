Instructions

  1. Allow the browser to access your MIDI devices.
  2. Connect your Enzo to the MIDI adapter.
  3. Select input & output devices and MIDI channel.
  4. Move a knob on your Enzo, the corresponding on-screen control must move accordingly.
    • To move the knobs, use the mouse.
    • Click and drag over the knob. You can also use the mousewheel.

Keyboard shortcuts:

  • ESC: close dialogs and settings
  • 0..9: presets 1..10
  • Right, +: next preset
  • Left, -: previous preset
  • Alt: keep pressed to view raw values
  • SPACE BAR: Bypass on/off
  • TAB: toggle EXP values
  • Shift: show EXP values
  • I: Init
  • R: Randomize
  • D,A,M,P: Dry, Arp, Mono, Poly
  • W: Sawtooth wave
  • Q: Square wave
  • T: TAP
  • PageUp: EXP max
  • PageDn: EXP min
  • C, V: animate pitch
  • F, G: animate filter
  • H, J: animate filter Q
  • Y, X: animate mix
  • K, L: animate delay level
  • B, N: animate sustain
  • DEL: animate sustain to 0
  • Up, Dn: animate exp
  • Z, U: animate ring mod
  • -, +: animate modulation
  • O: animate portamento
Use Ctrl to play animations until 50%.
